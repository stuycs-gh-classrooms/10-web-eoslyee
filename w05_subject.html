ABOUT

The Stuyvesant Panthers are Stuyvesant's varsity gymnastics team. Originally named the stuy Lemurs, but changed in 2020, the team has been around since 2016. It is currently coached by Mr. Vasken Choubaralian and Ms. Jenna Freytag.
